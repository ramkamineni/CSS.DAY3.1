TRAFFIC LIGHT SIGNALS



RED :
  In traffic lights, the red color universally signals "stop".
  It is used to instruct drivers, pedestrians, and other road users
  to halt and wait until the light changes to green or another designated signal
        
ORANGE :
In traffic lights, the Orange light means “get ready to stop,”
red is soon to follow. It also means caution, look both ways and give way to pedestrians at a crossing
